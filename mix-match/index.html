Black Women Are Fine Art

From how we adorn ourselves, our speech, to the structure of our cheekbones and fullness of our lips, Black women are the standard of beauty. Others may imitate but duplicating is simply not possible! This little web app is a love letter to Black women. Download what you made and share on Instagram with the hashtag #BWAFA.
